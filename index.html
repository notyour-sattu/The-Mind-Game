THE MIND GAME

The logic of the game is as follows: For example, the yellow button for level 1 flashed.
Then, at Level 2, the red button flashed.
you have to complete the level 2 by pressing the yellow button first and then the red button.
If the green button flashes in Level 3; first you have to press the button in level 1, then the button in level 2 and then the button in level 3.
In other words, you have to move forward by keeping the flashing buttons in the previous levels in mind.

For example ;
To pass level 4: Level 1 button then Level 2 button then Level 3 Button and Level 4 Button must be pressed.
